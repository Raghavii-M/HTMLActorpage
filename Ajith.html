Ajith

Ajith Kumar is a prominent Indian actor, predominantly working in Tamil cinema, known for blockbuster films like Aasai (his breakthrough) and Mankatha, and recognized for his versatile roles, philanthropic work (Blood Bank, Seva), and passion for motorsports, including Formula Racing; he's married to actress Shalini and is a celebrated star often called "Thala" or "Ultimate Star" by fans, with numerous awards and a huge following. Key Information: Born: May 1, 1970, in Hyderabad, India, to P. S. Mani and Mohini. Career Start: Began with small roles, debuting as a lead in Amaravathi (1993), followed by his breakthrough in Aasai (1995). Genres: Known for action, romance, thrillers, and dramas, starring in over 60 films. Awards: Recipient of several Vijay Awards, Filmfare Awards South, and Tamil Nadu State Film Awards. Motorsports: A passionate racer, he's competed in Formula championships (like Formula 2) and MRF Racing series. Personal Life: Married to actress Shalini (since 2000); they have a daughter, Anoushka, and a son, Adhik. Fanbase: Commands a massive following in South India, often referred to by titles like "Thala" (Leader). Key Films: Amaravathi (1993) Aasai (1995) Kadhal Kottai (1996) Vaali (1999) Villu (2009) Mankatha (2011) Arrambam (2013) Yennai Arindhaal (2015) Viswasam (2019) Valimai (2022) Thunivu (2023) Beyond Acting: Philanthropy: Runs an active blood donation and organ donation drive through his fan clubs, known as Ajith Kumar Welfare Association.
